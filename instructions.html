Instructions

Dear participant , please read the instructions before the experiment.

You have been assigned with a participant ID. Please login with your ID first.

Then please go to the 'Pre-Questionaire' and answer all the questions before the experiment.

After that, you can start the test. Click 'Experiment' Tab on the top and you will see the following table.

You are tasked to find content inside the pink box over all the open windows, and copy-paste the content to the Text Editor with either AutoComPaste (ACP) or traditional Ctrl C, Ctrl V technique.

Please read the instruction carefully which technique you have been required.

You are also required to do single paste or multiple paste according to the instruction.

For Multiple paste, please easte for 3 times.

After each trial, please click 'Next Task' button, you will proceed with next experiment condition.

After all 12 trials, you are also invited to fill in a post-experiment survey.

Thank you for your patiently reading the instruction. If you are ready for the experiment, please click 'Next' Button below.
